The Barristers' Palette & Brush Society

An outlet for law students to explore visual art in any form, from painting and drawing to digital art and photography.

About the Society

The Barristers' Palette & Brush Society celebrates the visual arts within the legal community. We believe that artistic expression enhances critical thinking, creativity, and provides a balanced perspective that enriches the study of law.
