üìö New Faculty Regulations Introduced

The Faculty of Law has implemented new academic regulations aimed at addressing concerns raised by stakeholders, including the Law Society. These changes introduce additional modules and adjustments to the semester structure.

While the new rules are intended to improve student preparedness, many students have reported feeling overwhelmed and confused. The added modules contribute to already packed schedules, and there is ongoing debate about whether increasing content is the most effective way to meet stakeholder expectations.
